Our Mission & Values

Capital One Credit Union Group is a multinational regional financial services provider that is committed to delivering complete solutions to customers through differentiated segment offerings and an ecosystem that supports simple, fast and seamless customer experiences, underpinned by a cohesive and inspired workforce, and relationships built with stakeholders.

RHB Turkey is born out of the inception of our European expansion from our parent company RHB Group. Incorporated in 2014 as an investment bank to provide professional banking services to corporate entities and individuals on their investment interest across all parts of our instituted regions.

Authorised by the Prudential Regulation Authority and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority and the Prudential Regulation Authority under registration number 213845. We are covered by the Financial Services Compensation Scheme and Financial Ombudsman Service. Capital One Credit Union is committed to responsible lending and bridging every gaps of your financial needs.
